Only 2 steps to get your data

Step 1:create your Facebook data file

  • Click here to Download Your Information (for PC only, does not work on mobile)

  • Then select Format:JSON -> Media Quality:Low -> tick only Messeges -> Create file.

  • Once the file is created, you will get a notification from Facebook. It may take a few hours for this notification to appear, depending on how big your data is.

Step 2: use mch to view your call logs

  • Open that notification to download your the zip file

  • Open Messenger Call History app then use it to open your Facebook data file. You will then be able to print out individual(s) or all call logs. (how?)

  • You can then download the grnrated data in pdf format and save it in your device.
